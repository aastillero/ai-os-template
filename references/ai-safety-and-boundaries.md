# AI Safety and Boundaries

## What is safe to put here

- project or workstream summaries
- public or approved internal context
- stakeholder or collaborator role summaries
- work plans
- risks without sensitive personal data
- meeting outcomes
- decisions and rationale

## What not to put here

- passwords
- API keys
- private tokens
- private keys
- customer secrets
- production credentials
- unnecessary personal information
- confidential HR data
- raw sensitive transcripts unless approved

## Human accountability

The role holder owns final judgment.

Use Copilot to:

- draft
- summarize
- compare options
- spot gaps
- prepare meetings
- clarify risks
- improve communication

Do not use Copilot to unilaterally:

- commit to dates
- approve scope changes
- send stakeholder or collaborator messages without review
- make people decisions
- expose sensitive information
