# Weekly Review

Use this at the end of the week or before a weekly stakeholder/collaborator update.

## Suggested prompt

```text
Using the active role profile and active project/workstream folders in this workspace, prepare my weekly review.

For each active project or workstream, use its 4C folders and summarize:
- current status: green / amber / red
- what changed this week
- key accomplishments
- risks and blockers
- decisions needed
- connection changes: stakeholders, dependencies, systems, or communication paths
- capability opportunities: repeated workflows or prompts that should be improved
- upcoming cadence checkpoints or milestones
- stakeholder or collaborator messages needed

Then give me a prioritized list of actions for next week.
```

## Weekly review checklist

- Are project `context/` files current?
- Are stakeholder and dependency `connections/` current?
- Are reusable `capabilities/` or prompts worth improving?
- Are cadence checkpoints still useful?
- Are RAID items updated?
- Are decisions captured with reasons?
- Are stakeholder/collaborator asks explicit?
- Are next milestones still realistic?
- Did any risk become an issue?
- Is there anything the team should stop doing?
