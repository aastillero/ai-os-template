## Meeting rhythms

| Meeting | Cadence | Purpose | Required output |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Daily sync | Daily | Team sync and blockers | Blockers and next actions |
| Work review | Weekly | Health, progress, and decisions | Status, risks, decisions |
| Stakeholder update | Weekly / as needed | Alignment and escalation | Clear status and asks |

## Definition of healthy work

Work is healthy when:

- outcomes are understood
- timeline or cadence is realistic
- risks are visible
- decisions are recorded
- blockers have owners
- stakeholders or collaborators are aligned
- the team knows what matters next

## Escalation norms

Escalate when:

- a decision is blocked
- a dependency threatens timeline or outcome
- scope changes without trade-off
- risk becomes an issue
- capacity no longer matches expectations
