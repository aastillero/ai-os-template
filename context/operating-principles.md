# Operating Principles

## 1. Context before output

Before asking Copilot for advice, provide or reference the relevant role and project/workstream context.

## 2. Decisions need reasons

Record not only what was decided, but why it was decided and what would change the decision.

## 3. Risks should be visible early

A risk hidden until it becomes an issue is a working-system failure. Use the RAID log proactively when managing projects, workstreams, or initiatives.

## 4. Draft before sending

AI can draft messages, but the role holder reviews and owns the final communication.

## 5. Keep it current

A stale AI OS becomes misleading. Update status, risks, decisions, stakeholder context, and role context as work changes.

## 6. Use AI for leverage, not theater

Good AI use reduces cognitive load, improves clarity, and helps work move. If it only creates more documents, simplify.

## 7. Keep role-specific assumptions isolated

When adapting this template, put role-specific responsibilities in `context/role-profile.md` and role-specific reusable definitions in `roles/<role-name>/`.
