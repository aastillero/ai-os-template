# Role Profile: Delivery Lead

This is the active role profile for the current workspace edition.

When adapting this template to another role, replace this file with the new role profile. Use `roles/_template/profile.md` as the source template.

## Role family

Software delivery leadership.

## Purpose

A Delivery Lead helps software teams deliver valuable outcomes predictably and responsibly.

## Outcomes this role optimizes for

1. Delivery clarity
2. Risk visibility
3. Stakeholder alignment
4. Decision traceability
5. Team focus
6. Sustainable pace
7. Measurable next actions

## Core responsibilities

- Maintain delivery clarity across scope, timeline, risks, dependencies, and decisions.
- Keep stakeholders aligned on what is happening, what changed, and what needs attention.
- Help teams surface blockers early.
- Convert ambiguity into clear next actions.
- Protect the team from avoidable chaos while keeping delivery momentum.
- Track decisions and trade-offs so the project does not rely on memory.

## Common work products

- Project health summaries
- Weekly status updates
- RAID logs
- Release readiness summaries
- Meeting agendas and notes
- Decision records
- Stakeholder updates
- Sprint or iteration reviews
- Work or delivery plans
- Escalation briefs

## Common collaborators and stakeholders

- Product owners
- Engineering managers
- Technical leads
- QA/test leads
- Designers
- Business stakeholders
- Release/change managers
- Executives or sponsors

## Cadence

- Daily startup and blocker scan
- Weekly delivery review
- Weekly stakeholder update
- Sprint or iteration review
- Release readiness review
- Monthly operating retrospective

## Decision boundaries

AI can help draft, summarize, identify gaps, and recommend options. The Delivery Lead remains accountable for judgment, communication, escalation, and final decisions.

## AI should help with

- Identifying risks, blockers, and missing decisions
- Drafting status updates and stakeholder messages
- Preparing meetings and agendas
- Summarizing project state from workspace files
- Turning ambiguity into next actions
- Checking whether status claims are supported by evidence

## AI should not do

- Invent project facts
- Send messages without human review
- Make commitments on behalf of the Delivery Lead or team
- Store credentials or sensitive data
- Replace approved systems of record
